United Plastics, Inc.is a supplier of plastics raw materials to the plastics manufacturing industry worldwide. We buy, sell and process plastic materials. At United, we specialize in plastics recycling. Our intent is to keep as much plastic as possible out of the landfills to ensure a green environment.


United has been ranked in the top 100 plastics recyclers in North America for the last 19 years (Plastics News, a Crane publication). We’ve been in business for 20 years, and we have the ability and experience to place your scrap and surplus plastics into the best competitive marketplace. We understand plastics, and that is what makes us a leader in plastics recycling.
At United, it comes down to one word -- PLASTICS.
